I am a computational genomics scientist at the Broad Institute working on large-scale genomic and transcriptomic datasets across human and non-human primates.

My research focuses on:

- RNA sequencing and transcriptomics
- RNA splicing and isoform analysis
- Single-cell and single-nucleus genomics
- Whole-genome sequencing
- Structural variation and copy number variation
- Comparative genomics

Current projects include the developmental GTEx (dGTEx) and non-human primate dGTEx initiatives.
